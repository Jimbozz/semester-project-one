FAQ's

Read through our FAQ’s to see if they can answer any of your questions. Otherwise feel free to use our contact form or reach out to us at hello@cosm.com

Admission is always free at the Community of Oslo Science Museum. Passes are required for all visitors regardless of age, and every member of your party must have pass. Passes may not be sold or transferred, are valid only for the issued date, and time and will be void if altered.

A limited number of wheelchairs are available free of charge for use while visiting the museum. To help maintain the sanitation of the wheelchairs, notify us before your visit. Visitors may ask for assistance from security officers at the entrance.

Free Wi-Fi access is available in most areas of the building. Look for the cosm-visitor network and follow the instructions.

Video cameras and flash photography are permitted in the museum, with the exception of a few exhibitions that are clearly marked. Use of tripods, monopods, and selfie sticks are not permitted. News photographers and videographers must arrange visits with the Office of Communications and Marketing by calling 1-562-867-5309
